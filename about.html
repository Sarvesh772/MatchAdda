About

Why MatchAdda exists.

MatchAdda is built for cricket fans who want a simple, fast, and clean place to follow the IPL season without clutter. The goal is to keep schedules, team pages, standings, and fan guides easy to scan on mobile and desktop.

Independent by design

MatchAdda is an unofficial fan website. It is designed to summarise public cricket information in a fast, readable format without pretending to be an official league property.

Built for quick use

The site focuses on practical pages that fans actually use: schedule, points table, teams, guides, and clear updates when matches are live or completed.

Made for all devices

MatchAdda is intentionally built as a lightweight static site so it works smoothly on phones, tablets, laptops, and GitHub Pages style hosting.
