About

MatchAdda kyun bana.

MatchAdda un cricket fans ke liye bana hai jo IPL season ko bina clutter ke simple, fast aur clean tareeke se follow karna chahte hain. Goal ye hai ki schedule, team pages, standings aur fan guides mobile aur desktop dono par easy to scan rahein.

Design se independent

MatchAdda ek unofficial fan website hai. Ye public cricket info ko fast aur readable format me dikhati hai bina official league property jaisa pretend kiye.

Quick use ke liye bana

Site un pages par focus karti hai jo fans sach me use karte hain: schedule, points table, teams, guides, aur clear updates jab match live ya complete ho.

Har device ke liye ready

MatchAdda ko intentionally lightweight static site ki tarah banaya gaya hai taaki ye phones, tablets, laptops aur GitHub Pages jaisi hosting par smooth chale.
