Legal

Clear, simple aur fan-site friendly.

MatchAdda fans ke liye ek independent informational cricket website hai. Ye koi official IPL, BCCI, franchise, broadcaster, sponsor, fantasy, merchandise ya ticketing platform nahi hai.

Independent website notice

MatchAdda season se judi general informational content deti hai. Team names aur tournament references sirf public sporting information ko identify karne ke liye use hote hain. Website par official logos, artwork, score graphics, photos, clips ya copied design elements use nahi hone chahiye.

Accuracy notice

Match dates, venues, start times aur standings change ho sakte hain. Visitors ko important details official league, franchise, broadcaster ya ticketing sources se verify kar leni chahiye.

Branding aur IP caution

Risk kam rakhne ke liye site name, logo aur domain ko independent rakho. Aise domains ya social handles avoid karo jo website ko official dikhayein, aur copied league assets ke around sponsorships bina legal clearance ke mat becho.

Privacy basics

MatchAdda ka ye static version forms, accounts ya user dashboards ke through intentionally personal information collect nahi karta.
