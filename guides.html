Useful cricket explainers for new and regular fans.

How the points table works

Each win gives a team 2 points. If teams are tied on points, net run rate becomes the key separator. The top four sides after the league stage qualify for the playoffs.

What to watch in the first two weeks

Early momentum, overseas player balance, home advantage, and bowling performance in pressure overs usually shape the first big narratives of the season.

Why net run rate suddenly becomes huge

Late in the league phase, teams can finish level on points. A strong NRR can be the difference between making the playoffs and missing out.

Best way to follow the season on one site

Start with schedule, check team snapshots, then visit the points table after every matchday. This simple journey is exactly what MatchAdda is designed for.
