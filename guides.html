Guides

Naye aur regular fans ke liye kaam ki cricket guides.

Ye guides site ko useful banati hain bina copied graphics ya official assets par depend kiye. Saath hi casual users ko site par zyada der tak useful cheezein milti rehti hain.

Points table kaise kaam karti hai

Har jeet par team ko 2 points milte hain. Agar teams points par tie ho jayein, to net run rate sabse important separator ban jata hai. League stage ke baad top four teams playoffs me jaati hain.

Pehle do hafton me kis cheez par nazar rakho

Early momentum, overseas player balance, home advantage, aur pressure overs me bowling performance season ki pehli badi stories banate hain.

Net run rate itni important kyun ho jati hai

League phase ke end me teams points par level finish kar sakti hain. Strong NRR playoffs me pahunchne aur bahar reh jane ke beech farq bana sakti hai.

Ek hi site par season ko best tareeke se kaise follow karein

Schedule se start karo, team snapshots dekho, phir har matchday ke baad points table check karo. Ye simple flow hi MatchAdda ka main design idea hai.
