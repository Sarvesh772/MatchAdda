FAQ

Visitors ke liye quick answers.

Ye answers visitors ko samjhate hain ki MatchAdda kya karta hai, score updates kaise kaam karte hain, aur kuch info ball-by-ball ke bajay match ke baad kyun update hoti hai.

Kya MatchAdda live scores ball-by-ball update karta hai?

Nahi. Live score updates hamesha ball-by-ball nahi hongi. Zarurat padne par final scores aur results match ke baad update kiye jate hain.

Kya MatchAdda official IPL ya BCCI website hai?

Nahi. MatchAdda ek independent cricket fan website hai aur IPL, BCCI, franchises ya broadcasters se affiliated nahi hai.

Kya schedule ya result info change ho sakti hai?

Haan. Match timings, venues aur dusri details change ho sakti hain. Important info hamesha official sources se verify karni chahiye.

Site simple pages par hi focus kyun karti hai?

Goal speed aur clarity hai. MatchAdda quick checks ke liye bani hai, khaaskar mobile devices aur slower connections par.
