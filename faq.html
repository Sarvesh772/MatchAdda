FAQ

Quick answers for visitors.

These answers help visitors understand what MatchAdda does, how score updates work, and why some information may be updated after the end of a match instead of ball-by-ball.

Does MatchAdda update live scores ball-by-ball?

No. Live score updates may not always be ball-by-ball. Final scores and results are updated after matches when needed.

Is MatchAdda an official IPL or BCCI website?

No. MatchAdda is an independent cricket fan website and is not affiliated with the IPL, BCCI, franchises, or broadcasters.

Can schedule or result information change?

Yes. Match timings, venues, and other details can change. Important information should always be verified from official sources.

Why does the site focus on simple pages?

The goal is speed and clarity. MatchAdda is made for quick checks, especially on mobile devices and slower connections.
